Peanut Butter Protein Balls Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Protein powder
  • Peanut butter
  • maple syrup/honey

Method

STEP 1 - Make the dough: Add all the ingredients into a mixing bowl and mix until a thick batter remains. If using chocolate chips, fold them through at the end.

STEP 2 - Shape into balls: Lightly wet your hands and roll out small balls and place them on a plate lined with parchment paper.

STEP 3 - Chill: Place the peanut butter protein balls in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to firm up.

Note: Leftovers are best stored in an airtight container in the fridge. They will keep well for up to four weeks.
