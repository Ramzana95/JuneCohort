Protein Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 banana
  • 75g oats
  • 2 tbsp milk (any milk will do)
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • Protein powder
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • coconut oil (to fry)
  • maple syrup and berries or any topping of your choice (to serve)

Method

STEP 1 - Put the banana, oats, eggs, milk, baking powder, cinnamon, and protein powder in a blender and blend until smooth. Check that oats everything is smooth, if not, blend for another minute.

STEP 2 - Heat a small amount of oil in a pan and pour in 2-3 rounds of batter, leaving a little space between each to spread.

STEP 3 - Cook for 1-2 minutes, until bubbles start to appear on the surface and the underside is golden. Flip over and cook for another minute until cooked through.

STEP 4 - Transfer to a warmed oven and repeat with the remaining batter. Serve in stacks with any topping of your choice.
